About this event

Moving beyond swipes and surface connections to date with clarity and purpose.

In a world of endless options and instant gratification, it's easy to lose sight of what we actually want. Dating can feel like a performance, a numbers game, or simply exhausting. But what if we approached it differently?

Dating intentionally isn't about following rules or strategies—it's about getting clear on your values, understanding your patterns, and showing up authentically. It's about making choices that align with who you are and what you're genuinely looking for, rather than just going through the motions.

This conversation will explore:

  • How to define what you truly want (beyond the checklist)
  • Recognizing and shifting unhelpful dating patterns
  • Setting boundaries that honor your energy and values
  • Moving from reactive to intentional in your dating life
  • Building authentic connections in a culture of surface-level interactions
  • Balancing openness with discernment

Whether you're actively dating, taking a break to reset, or curious about approaching relationships more mindfully, this event offers space for reflection, clarity, and honest conversation.
