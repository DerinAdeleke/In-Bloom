About this event

How do we express and receive love when so much of our connection happens through screens?

In an era defined by texts, voice notes, video calls, and curated posts, the ways we communicate affection have fundamentally shifted. What gets lost in translation? What new forms of intimacy emerge? And how do we stay present with each other when digital distraction is always one tap away?

This conversation explores the texture of modern love languages—not just words of affirmation or quality time, but the small gestures that matter in our hybrid lives. We'll discuss:

  • How technology shapes the way we give and receive love
  • The gap between digital availability and emotional presence
  • Building rituals of connection in long-distance and digitally-mediated relationships
  • Recognizing when your partner's love language doesn't translate online

Whether you're navigating a new relationship, redefining an established one, or simply curious about the evolution of intimacy, this is a space for honest reflection and community.
