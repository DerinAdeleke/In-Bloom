About In Bloom

A curated space for thoughtful conversations about modern love, dating, and intimacy

What is In Bloom?

In Bloom is a conversation series that brings together curious, thoughtful people to explore the complexities of modern relationships.

We live in an era of unprecedented connection and paradoxical loneliness. Dating apps promise infinite choice while making it harder to commit. We're hyper-aware of our communication patterns yet still struggle to be vulnerable. Technology offers new ways to stay close, but also new ways to hide.

In Bloom creates a space to sit with these tensions—not to solve them, but to examine them together with honesty, nuance, and care. Through intimate events featuring therapists, writers, researchers, and cultural observers, we explore what it means to love, connect, and show up for each other in the digital age.

This is not a networking event or a singles mixer. It's a salon, a forum, a place for reflection. We believe that understanding our relationships more deeply helps us show up more fully—to partners, to friends, to ourselves.

Why we exist

Conversations about modern love often fall into two camps: overly clinical advice or dismissive generalizations about "kids these days." We wanted something different.

In Bloom was born from a belief that we need spaces that take relationships seriously—as sites of growth, challenge, joy, and meaning. We need conversations that are smart without being academic, personal without being prescriptive, and grounded in real experiences without flattening them into trends.

We exist to help people feel less alone in their questions and more equipped to navigate the beautiful, messy work of connection.

What to expect

Intimate settings

Our events are capped at 40–50 people to foster genuine conversation. This isn't a lecture hall; it's a living room.

Thoughtful speakers

We feature therapists, authors, researchers, and cultural observers who bring both expertise and empathy. Our speakers aren't just knowledgeable—they're curious.

Guided discussions

Each event includes time for group dialogue. We use prompts and breakout conversations to help attendees reflect and connect, but participation is always optional.

Welcoming tone

Whether you're single, partnered, dating, or figuring it out, you belong here. We welcome all relationship structures, orientations, and lived experiences.
