Frequently asked questions

Can I suggest a topic for an upcoming event?

Absolutely! We're always looking for new angles and perspectives. Send us your ideas at hello@inbloom.example.

Do you offer private or corporate events?

We occasionally collaborate on custom events for organizations, workplaces, or private groups. Reach out to discuss your needs: partners@inbloom.example.

Are events recorded or livestreamed?

We occasionally record events for our community, but we prioritize privacy and consent. Attendees are always notified in advance if an event will be recorded.

What if I can't afford a ticket?

We believe these conversations should be accessible. We offer sliding scale pricing and a limited number of free tickets for each event. Email us at hello@inbloom.example and we'll work something out.
